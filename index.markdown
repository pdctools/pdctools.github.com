## Welcome! ##

> ![kitteh!](http://placekitten.com/600/401)

Welcome to the home of `pdctools`, a set of tools aimed to ease the
installation and the management of a Linux-based domain controller.

Please [find out more](/about.html) on the project itself, learn more of it by
[its documentation](/documentation.html), check out [news](/news.html) on the
project, or simply [listen to rants](/blog.html) on the project and its
implementation.

If you feel in the verve of contributing with ideas or developing
skills, please [contact](/contacts.html) the developers, check out [bug
reports and requests](/bugs.html) or browse the [sources](/sources.html).
